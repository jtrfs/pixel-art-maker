Instructions - How to draw

  1. Set the size of the Canvas and click the Create the Canvas button
  2. Pick a colour by clicking on the black stripe
  3. Now, you can click individual cells to colour them
  4. By doubleclicking you can erase a cell in question
  5. If you find the grid lines obtrusive, hit the Grid ON/OFF button
  6. If you don't like your work, erase it by the Rub Off the Canvas button
  7. To bring this panel back on, click the icon or reload the page
